Getting Started

How do I set up a workout?

Tap the + button in the top-left corner to open workout settings. You have two options:

  • Manual setup: Adjust sets, reps, work time, and rest time by swiping up or down on each value
  • Use a preset: Tap the list icon to choose from built-in workout templates (Tabata, HIIT, Strength, and more)

Tap the checkmark to confirm and start your workout.

How do I start the timer?

Tap the play button or tap directly on the timer display. The app will show a countdown sequence before starting your workout.

How do I reset the timer?

You can reset the timer by:

  • Double-tap anywhere on the screen
  • Double-tap or long-press the play/pause button
  • Tap the reset button (circular arrow) when a workout is configured

How do I change the theme?

Tap the paintpalette icon in the top-right corner to open the theme picker. Choose from 13 beautiful themes:

  • Dark themes: Matrix, Ocean, Aurora, Sky, Lavender, Sunset, Peach
  • Light themes: Ocean Light, Sky Light, Lavender Light, Sunset Light, Peach Light, Aurora Light

The background gradient animates smoothly, rotating continuously for a dynamic look.

Workout Phases

During your workout, you'll see these phases:

  • GET READY - Preparation countdown
  • SET - Almost time to begin
  • GO - Start your exercise!
  • WORKOUT - Active work period
  • REST - Recovery between reps
  • COMPLETE - You've finished!

Troubleshooting

The timer isn't starting

Make sure you've tapped the play button or timer display. If you have a workout configured, the countdown sequence will play first.

I can't see workout indicators

Set and rep indicators only appear after configuring a workout using the + button and confirming with the checkmark.

I can't access my history or stats

History, statistics, and achievements are premium features. Tap the clock icon and then "Unlock" to upgrade to Premium.

My purchase didn't go through

If your purchase didn't complete:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're signed in to the App Store
  • Try the "Restore Purchases" button if you've previously purchased
  • Restart the app and try again
