Getting Started

How do I set up a workout?

Tap the + button in the top-left corner to open workout settings. Adjust your sets, reps, work time, and rest time by swiping up or down on each value. Tap the checkmark to confirm.

How do I start the timer?

Tap the play button or tap directly on the timer display. The app will show a countdown sequence before starting your workout.

How do I reset the timer?

You can reset the timer by:

  • Double-tap anywhere on the screen
  • Double-tap or long-press the play/pause button
  • Tap the reset button when a workout is configured

Workout Phases

During your workout, you'll see these phases:

  • GET READY - Preparation countdown
  • SET - Almost time to begin
  • GO - Start your exercise!
  • WORKOUT - Active work period
  • REST - Recovery between reps
  • COMPLETE - You've finished!

Features

  • Customizable sets, reps, work time, and rest time
  • Visual countdown sequence
  • Set and rep progress indicators
  • Works in portrait and landscape
  • Optimized for iPhone and iPad
  • Settings automatically saved

Troubleshooting

The timer isn't starting

Make sure you've tapped the play button or timer display. If you have a workout configured, the countdown sequence will play first.

I can't see workout indicators

Set and rep indicators only appear after configuring a workout using the + button and confirming with the checkmark.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Data Not Collected

Fatbelly is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your workout data is your business, not ours.

Data Collection

Fatbelly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

Data Type Collected? Details
Personal Information No No name, email, or account required
Health & Fitness Data No No workout data leaves your device
Usage Analytics No No tracking or analytics
Location Data No Location is never accessed
Device Identifiers No No device IDs collected
Advertising Data No No ads, no ad tracking

Local Storage

Your workout settings (sets, reps, work time, rest time) are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Fatbelly does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. The app operates entirely offline.

External Links

The app contains an optional tip link that, when tapped by the user, opens an external payment service (Revolut) in Safari. This is user-initiated and subject to Revolut's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Fatbelly does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. However, our commitment to not collecting your data will remain unchanged.
